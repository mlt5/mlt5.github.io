About how long was this experiment run for?
SOLUTION: 4 months

What action types are recorded in this dataset? Select all that apply.
SOLUTION:
  • view
  • click

Why would we use click through rate instead of number of clicks to compare the performances of control and experiment pages? (Choose all that apply.)
SOLUTION:
  • The control and experiment pages could have different numbers of total visitors
  • More total clicks could occur in one version, even if there is a greater percentage of clicks in the other version.

Choose the correct definition for the click through rate (CTR) in this experiment.
SOLUTION: The number of unique visitors who click at least once divided by the number of unique visitors who view the page

10. [Quiz] Hierarchical clustering

1

Which linkage method in Hierarchical clustering is more prone to result in elongated shapes that are not necessarily compact or circular?

SOLUTION: Single linkage

Which linkage method in hierarchical clustering works by merging clusters that lead to the least increase in variance in the clusters after merging?

SOLUTION: Ward's method
